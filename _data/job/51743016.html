OVERVIEW

Linkly has been Australia's leading supplier of payment integration solutions for over 20 years.  Our rock-solid middleware powers over 200,000 terminals and over two billion transactions annually and our applications support over 500 POS applications.

Linkly is a private company, based in Sydney's CBD with offices in Melbourne and Singapore. Our business was formed in 1998, under the PC-EFTPOS name, and we have built our reputation through seamless integration of payment terminals and POS systems.  Our clients range from major retailers such as Woolworths, David Jones, Myer, JB Hi-Fi and Bunnings, right through to single-terminal corner stores.

More recently, we expanded our capability through the acquisition of the Premier Technologies / MerchantSuite e-commerce platform. The acquisition propels Linkly into the online and telephone payment space and solidifies our position as a leader in end-to-end payments and Omni-channel solutions.

The move means that Linkly will be able to handle e-commerce payments alongside its leading in-store payment products, providing a suite of solutions that are able to exceed our client's payment needs across Australia, New Zealand and Asia - resulting in a seamless user experience that saves time and money.

We are driven by a goal to build close working partnerships with our clients, who include merchants, merchant acquirers and POS vendors.  Through our experience, we aim to add value to every aspect of the payment eco-system. Our aim is to be the total purchase pathway provider that seamlessly reduces the touch points within the payment supply-chain. We give our clients the peace of mind to focus on their core business. From platform to payment, we connect all the dots.

PURPOSE OF THE POSITION

To continually increase revenue, loyalty, and usability of our products.  The Product Manager must drive customer entanglement and create lifetime value by continually evolving the product offering.

WHAT YOU'LL DO

  • Set a product vision, strategy and roadmap that is differentiated and relevant that delivers unique value based on customer demands.
  • Define requirements and ensure on time delivery
  • Provide support to development teams, sales & marketing, and helpdesk.
  • Communicating the product vision and roadmap and keeping everyone informed of updates.
  • Oversee the end-to-end product lifecycle including concept, design, forecasting, development, testing, promotion, quality, support, and finally product end life.
  • Develop product positioning
  • Drive Customer Success Outcomes
  • Ensure product documentation is kept up to date

WHAT YOU'LL BRING WITH YOU

  • 5+ years' experience in product management or development.
  • A tertiary qualification in a Business/Technology related discipline.
  • Strong focus on customer experience.
  • An understanding of how to develop and apply quality systems and processes.
  • Strong investigative/analytical and problem-solving skills.
  • Enthusiastic and creative leader with the ability to inspire others.
  • Thrives in a multi-tasking environment and can adjust priorities on the fly.
  • High level planning and organising skills to ensure work priorities are completed on time and within budget.
  • Ability to learn about our products and our target market quickly.
  • Demonstrated ability to drive continuous value of product offerings.

GENERAL

  • Proven skills in strategy development & execution and commercial acumen
  • Computer literacy – Word, PowerPoint, Excel, and CRM tools such as HubSpot
  • Strong business acumen with an ability to "solve" with the overall business needs in mind
  • Strong interpersonal skills, with the ability to engage stakeholders and customers
  • Excellent written and verbal communication skills
  • Excellent communication and presentation skills to engage high level stakeholders
  • Ability to work collaboratively across the company
